Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert celcius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing 'CelciusTemp' with your temperature in celcius: (CelciusTemp * 9/5 + 32)


Weight




To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing 'kg' with your weight in kilogram: kg * 2.205


Distance




To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing 'km' with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
